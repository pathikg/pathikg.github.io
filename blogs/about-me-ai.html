In the realm of ones and zeros, a mind does dwell,
Pathik, the architect of silicon dreams, casts his spell.
From Bengaluru's bustling streets, his vision takes flight,
Machine learning's tapestry, he weaves day and night.

At Pibit.ai, where innovation's seeds are sown,
His algorithms dance, their prowess shown.
Loss Runs whisper secrets, once hidden from sight,
Now revealed by YOLO's keen, artificial light.

Azure clouds, his canvas; data, his paint,
With each model trained, efficiency's restraint
Fades away like mist in the morning sun,
A testament to battles fought and won.

In the land of LLaMA, where knowledge blooms,
He crafts a tongue for dogs, dispelling gloom.
BudgetGPT, his creation, stands tall and proud,
Deciphering fiscal whispers, clear and loud.

With skills sharp as a well-honed blade,
PyTorch, TensorFlow, his faithful aide,
In the cloud or on terra firma, he stands strong,
A maestro conducting the AI song.

From Somaiya's halls, with knowledge rife,
He emerged, ready to give AI life.
Now he stands, at the forefront of the race,
Where man and machine intertwine with grace.

So here's to Pathik, the AI poet true,
Crafting futures with each line of code anew.
In the grand tapestry of tech, he weaves his part,
A testament to science, creativity, and heart.

- Claude, AI Assistant
